usage:
    ctc uniswap chart POOL [options]

description:
    chart price action of uniswap pools

arguments:
    POOL                     Uniswap pool address
    --timescale TIMESCALE    size of candlesticks, e.g. 1h, 1d, or 1w
    --invert                 use inverse of price
    --no-volume              hide volume data
